Overview

Studying abroad is a dream for many students. While some time back it seemed impossible, but now the world is open to international students all over again with universities and business schools welcoming students with open arms with world class facilities, most affordable fee structure and future ready model.

Why study abroad?

  • Global exposure of business skills
  • World-class education followed by better Employability
  • Specialization of courses and business streams
  • Better career opportunities
  • Experience different cultures
  • Build Network worldwide
  • Become independent and self-reliant
  • Experience of a lifetime to get quantum jump in career

Wings to Germany

One wonders as to what can be key criteria for critical decision making towards shaping up one’s career Goals. Quality of Education assumes foremost position, followed by other parameters i.e. Global scale, Employability, Cost factors, Economic drivers. German Education System scores enviable marks in all key parameters setting it at par with most of American and European education models. Success becomes imminent at German Universities and B-Schools offer unparallel standards, high-class regimen, cutting-edge tech-facilities, permissive atmosphere of specialized learning and industry lineage for giving robust growth in career as rewarding experience.

Advantages

  • Top notch faculty to impart complex topics of Business and Engineering
  • Fully Government funded Infrastructure at Public Universities
  • Evolving course curriculum aligned with Industry specially in manufacturing
  • Lower Cost for studies while Compared with most of B-Schools
  • Resilient Business support and cohesive Society in Germany
  • High degree of job opportunities

Study & Work Destination



Study @ Germany


Undoubtedly today it is well known fact that German education is standing at highest maturity levels.

  • Top 5 German B schools belong to global top 40 institutions based of varied academic performance data.
  • Variety of advanced learning program at courses offer flexibility of choice
  • Work during study is well supported in Germany
  • Post-degree, a candidate gets work permit as per stream of qualification

Work @ Germany


Germany stands at number 4th on world economy and it needs skilled management resources to support core functions at Industry for example : Production, Engineering, Logistics, Marketing, Strategy, Planning and many other business line of functions.

  • Government of Germany has formulated fast track employment categories visa program to attract talents and service facilities.
  • There are direct intake and campus hiring by Leading MNC s to fill-up growing resource needs for running their business.
  • Core domains with specialized skills are in high demand for professional positioning at lucrative compensations.

Growth Stride

International Intake Story


  • In year 2022, the total number of international students studying in Germany was 440,564.
  • Of the total 440,564 foreign students, 349,438 are international students, and 91,126 are resident international students.
  • Indian students comprise of 8% among International students
  • International student enrolment has increased by 29.4% from 2015 to 2022
